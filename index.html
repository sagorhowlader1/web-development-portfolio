About Me

A frontend developer builds the user-facing part of websites and applications, transforming design mockups into interactive, functional, and responsive interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They ensure websites are accessible, fast, and work across various browsers and devices. Frontend developers turn UI/UX designs into code, often using Figma or Adobe XD, and then use CSS frameworks to make them responsive, and JavaScript to make them dynamic. They work closely with backend developers to integrate APIs and ensure smooth data flow.
